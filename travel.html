Travel

The Dell Lea is located at 81 Pleasant Street in Chichester, NH. The largest NASCAR race is held on Sunday but there are also qualifying races Saturday afternoon. We have been assured traffic is not too heavy on Saturday but Route 106 should be avoided as this is where the racetrack is located. Route 93 and 393 may also be backed up at this time.

We would like to invite everyone to arrive at the venue early to be able to enjoy the ceremony on time. Drinks and light snacks will be provided and you may arrive up to an hour early.

Helpful Apps: If you don’t already use Waze it is a great app that will help with avoiding traffic. It will reroute you the best way possible based on updates from other users who are stuck in traffic at that moment.
